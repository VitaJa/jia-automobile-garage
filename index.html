Who we are

Get to know about our company

Jia Automobile Garage is a locally registered company, founded 31st November 2015. Its headquarters is located in Arua-Uganda, on Avenue rd next to TotalEnergies, Arua District. It is under the supervision of the Managing Director, as indicated by the statutes. The Managing Director is at the same time in charge of the main garage.
We have experience working on all car-related problems big and small, keeping vehicles in good nick and offering excellent customer service - which is why we’re one of the best garages in Arua. Completing work at a time that suits you, we can service, MOT and offer car repairs in Arua, so you can get back on the road in no time!
